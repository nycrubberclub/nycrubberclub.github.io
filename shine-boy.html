Shine Boy ~ Get Your Gear Gleaming!

Get a full body shine, donate, and receive a keychain to show your support for the club!

The Shine Boy is a special role where a committee member helps fellow club members achieve a flawless, full-body rubber shine! Whether you're prepping for an event or just love that perfect polished look, our Shine Boy is here to help.

As a token of appreciation, members who make a donation of $20 or more to support NYCRC will receive an exclusive keychain — a stylish way to show your support for the club. Get shined up and give back to the community at the same time!
