HOW TO SUBSCRIBE TO NYC RUBBER CLUB EVENTS CALENDAR


Apple iOS (iPhone/iPad/iPod) Calendar

(instructions accurate as of iOS 14.6 @ Thu 24 Jun 2021)

Subscribe

  1. Open Calendar Settings
  2. Open Calendar Account Settings
  3. Add Subscribed Calendar
  4. On your home screen tap the 'Settings' > 'Calendar' > 'Accounts' > 'Add Account' > 'Other' > 'Add Subscribed Calendar'
  5. Paste the URL and click 'Next'
  6. You do not need a username or password. Change any settings you see fit before saving the calendar and click 'Next'

Make changes to your subscription or unsubscribe

  1. Toggle your view of the subscribed calendar by opening the Calendar app
  2. tap Calendars in the bottom center
  3. Tap a calendar name to either display or hide calendar events
  4. Tap a the (i) next to the calendar name to rename the calendar subscription, change the color, disable alerts or unsubcribe through deleting the calendar

Unsubscribe

  1. Toggle your view of the subscribed calendar by opening the Calendar app
  2. tap Calendars in the bottom center
  3. Tap a the (i) next to the calendar name
  4. tap "Delete Calendar" to unsubcribe

Google Calendar

(instructions accurate as of @ Fri 02 July 2021)

Subscribe

  1. Open google calendar https://calendar.google.com
  2. On the left sidebar, click the "+" icon next to Other calendars
  3. Click From URL
  4. Paste the URL and click 'Next'

Make changes to your subscription

  1. Open google calendar https://calendar.google.com
  2. Hover over the name of the calendar "NYC Rubber Club Events" and click the three vertical dots
  3. Click "Settings"
  4. Here you can configure notifications, customize the name of the calendar, etc...

Unsubscribe

  1. Open google calendar https://calendar.google.com
  2. Hover over the name of the calendar "NYC Rubber Club Events" and click the "X" to unsubscribe
