HOW TO SUBSCRIBE TO NYC RUBBERMEN EVENTS CALENDAR

Apple iOS (iPhone/iPad/iPod) Calendar

(instructions accurate as of iOS 14.6 @ Thu 24 Jun 2021)

Subscribe

  1. Open this page on your iPhone
  2. On your iPhone tap the Settings icon - PLACE HOLDER SCREENSHOT IMAGE
  3. Scroll down and select Mail, Contacts, Calendars - PLACE HOLDER SCREENSHOT IMAGE
  4. Select Add Account and select Other - PLACE HOLDER SCREENSHOT IMAGE
  5. Select the Add Subscribed Calendar option - PLACE HOLDER SCREENSHOT IMAGE
  6. Copy the URL (https://calendar.google.com/calendar/ical/jffshs6qd0nk6e002cf030fufk%40group.calendar.google.com/public/basic.ics) for the Rubbermen NYC Calendar
  7. Paste the URL in the Server box and click Next - PLACE HOLDER SCREENSHOT IMAGE
  8. Change any settings you see fit before saving the calendar. You do not need a username or password - PLACE HOLDER SCREENSHOT IMAGE

Make changes to your subscription or unsubscribe

  1. Toggle your view of the subscribed calendar by opening the Calendar app - PLACE HOLDER SCREENSHOT IMAGE
  2. tap Calendars in the bottom center - PLACE HOLDER SCREENSHOT IMAGE
  3. Tap a calendar name to either display or hide calendar events - PLACE HOLDER SCREENSHOT IMAGE
  4. Tap a the (i) next to the calendar name to rename the calendar subscription, change the color, disable alerts or unsubcribe through deleting the calendar - PLACE HOLDER SCREENSHOT IMAGE

Unsubscribe

  1. Toggle your view of the subscribed calendar by opening the Calendar app - PLACE HOLDER SCREENSHOT IMAGE
  2. tap Calendars in the bottom center - PLACE HOLDER SCREENSHOT IMAGE
  3. Tap a the (i) next to the calendar name
  4. tap "Delete Calendar" to unsubcribe - PLACE HOLDER SCREENSHOT IMAGE
