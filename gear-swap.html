Gear Swap

Keep track of your gear – NYCRC is not responsible for lost or stolen items.

The Gear Swap is a chance to buy, sell, or trade rubber, latex, and other fetish gear in a friendly and community-driven space. Please be respectful and ask before handling someone’s gear.

Here’s how it works:

Bring Gear to Sell/Trade

Package your item into a clear ziplock bag.

If your item is larger than a 2-gallon bag, we can put a string through a sleeve or hook to attach a label as well

Tag Your Gear

On a tag please write down the following information:

Name First Name & Last Initial / Alt. Name / Handle

Phone XXX-XXX-XXXX

Price $XXX

Donate Let us know if you want your item donated if not sold

Have a Rubber Club Committee member review your item(s). Then lay out your item(s) on the table and be nearby to do the exchange with the buyer.

Payments

Cash or digital payments are between buyer and seller.

Swap Gear

All sales and trades are final, please inspect items before purchasing. When you make a sale, please give your item's sold tag(s) and unsold tag(s) to one of the Rubber Club Committee members.

Questions? Comments? Concerns?

Got questions? Ask a committee member at the event or contact us. Happy swapping!
